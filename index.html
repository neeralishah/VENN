VOTEEN

Giving teenagers the opportunity to be prepared for the voting world.

Our Mission

As students who are soon to enter the political stage, we wanted to create an opportunity for young people to make informed decisions when it comes to voting. We decided to create an iPhone and Android application that allows users to create accounts and take quizzes to understand their political and social ideologies. This user friendly platform allows them to be matched to political candidates who align with their interests. From there, users can read about policies, money flow, and background related to those candidates.
